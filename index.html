Beat the Heat

Optimize Your Running Pace with the Temperature Adjustment Calculator!

The Temperature Adjustment Calculator enables you to adjust your target pace by factoring in the current temperature, allowing you to set realistic and achievable goals.

These pace adjustments are recommended for continuous runs, for repeat track workouts of a mile or below, you can half the adjustment time because the body has a chance to cool between repeats.
